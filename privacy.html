Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information AppCash collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email, username, and authentication identifiers.
  • Usage data: app events, device model, OS version, country/region, language, and IP address for security and fraud prevention.
  • Offer activity: completion status and points earned from tasks provided by third‑party partners.
  • Support: messages you send to our team.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the Service and personalize your experience.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or abuse.
  • Process rewards and maintain accurate balances.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security, and support.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies or local storage to remember preferences and keep you signed in. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Third‑Party Partners

App and survey offers are powered by third‑party providers who may collect information to verify task completion and prevent fraud. Their use of data is governed by their own policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share limited data with service providers and offer partners as necessary to operate the Service, comply with law, or protect rights and safety.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.

7. Your Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account information from within the app or by contacting support.
  • Opt out of non‑essential communications using in‑app settings or unsubscribe links.

8. Children’s Privacy

AppCash is not directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections are in place.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. Material changes will be communicated in the app or on this site.

12. Contact

Questions or requests? Contact us at support@getcashapp.com or for business inquiries business@getcashapp.com.
